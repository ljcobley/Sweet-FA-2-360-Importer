Export from FA Full-Time

This tool works when your active tab has an FA Full-Time widget open.
Open or switch to a tab like fulltime.thefa.com, then press Generate CSV.
Want to import into 360Player? Open a tab at app.360player.com and switch to the Import screen.

Import to 360Player

This tool works when your active tab is a 360Player team calendar.
Open or switch to a tab at app.360player.com, then press Run on current tab.
Need CSVs from FA Full-Time first? Open a tab at fulltime.thefa.com and switch to the Export screen.
